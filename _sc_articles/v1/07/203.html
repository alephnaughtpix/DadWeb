Have you ever saw a small light zip across the sky, and thought "My!!! I've seen a satellite!!! I wonder what that would look like in a 3D model rendered by a Java program!??!?!" Well, now you can find out thanks to NASA!!! They've done a 3D model of all satellites launched and you view it in some sort of new fangled Java window, and do all the usual "virtual reality" things like zoom and click around and so on... You can even do a simulated time-lapse of their orbits and see them go around the Earth very quickly, like a lot of midges!!!! So, coming from Scotland, I find it quite reassuringly familiar!!!!!

http://liftoff.msfc.nasa.gov/realtime/jtrack/3d/JTrack3d.html
