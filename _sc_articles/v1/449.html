Yes, it's true!!!!!!!!! MS reads the haven of propeller-head Linux geek nerds!!!!!!!! At least according to this memo leaked from MS-land on 31 Oct!!!!!! Hence the title "The Halloween Memo"!!!! This "memo" is about all that open source stuff, and what MS can do about it- and the answer appears to be "take over the Internet" and do really really complex software that no sane person would be able to emulates!!!!!!!! The only thing is... Linux Geeks are already way ahead of MS on that score!!!!!!!!!!!

http://www.tuxedo.org/~esr/halloween.html

When this info was posted on Slashdot on Saturday, the amount of responses to it caused a system overload!!!

http://slashdot.org/articles/98/11/02/1120257.shtml

Quite a few people on Slashdot wondered if this maybe a little hoax!!!!!! Well, it was confirmed by MS on Tuesday both in the print pages of the Wall Street Journal, and also on this story on news.com!!! Oddly enough, this is the only the first news story we've actually seen outside of Slashdot-land!!!!!! We didn't get a peep from ZD-Net or Wired when this first broke!!!!

http://www.news.com/News/Item/0,4,28215,00.html?st.ne.ni.lh
