We thought that you only got "Angry of Tumbridge-Wells" type letters in the UK, so we were pleased to see the following letter in the South China Morning Post!!!! Mind you, it's from Hong Kong, which is a former UK colony, so that may explain it!!!!!! Anyway, the letter bemoans the fact that MacDonalds are doing a "Snoopy" doll promotion, and loads of people are buying loadsa stuff from the "Big Mac" in order to get the Snoopy doll, then just dumping the food in the bin!!!!!!!! Sounds like a really great advert for MacDonalds, eh?!?!?!?!?
