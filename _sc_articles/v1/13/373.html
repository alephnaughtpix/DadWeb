So, how old are your records?!?!?! 10, 20, 30 year old?!?!?!? Well, some crew called the "Mayans" have a record that is over a thousand years old!!!!!! Obviously this dates from the time before gramophones, so it's not available on CD!!!!! In fact, it's in the extremely unportable form of a big cave!!!!! These clever Mayan dudes actually carved the inside parts of the cave in such as way that if you make a noise, the echo that comes back is actually a recording of a bird from the local area!!!!!!! Pretty neat, huh??!?!? I would like to see them try and emulate a record by Fatboy Slim, tho!!!!!
