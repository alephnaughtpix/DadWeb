Well, these guys certainly know how to "Fight the Power"!!!!! Only a month or two after they posted their entire "Bring the Noise 2000" album online in MP3 format just to annoy their record company, and then took it off after their record company got annoyed enough to get heavy with the lawyer people, they've now posted a new song, "Swindlers Lust"- in *MP4* format!!!!!!!!!!!

http://www.public-enemy.com/mp4/swinlust.html

But what's MP4!?!?! Is it the latest form of MPEG file?!?!?! In fact, no it isn't!!!!! It's just some company's proprietary format which they just happen to call "MP4", which by an odd coincidence just happens to sound exactly like what you would expect to call an MPEG-1 Layer 4 file, or an MPEG-4 file!!!!!

http://www.mp3.com/news/156.html

But that's not all!!!!!!!!!! A lot of people on Slashdot have noticed that PE's tune only available as Windows only executable file and are kicking up a bit of a fuss about it!!!!!!!! The executable apparently includes a player, but it's ironic that PE are slagging off commercialism in such a prinicipled fashion, whilst supporting the world's biggest software company and using a proprietary format!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

http://www.slashdot.org/articles/99/01/11/0847217.shtml

But the player wasn't the only executable!!!!!!!! At the time of writing (11/1/99), the website posted this warning:

If you downloaded Swindler's Lust MP4 today (1/7) between 3 p.m.and 5:15 p.m. EST, it may be infected with a virus. A new copy has been uploaded. P.E. apologizes for the oversight.

Woops!!!!!! Luckily, some compiled an MP3 version of the file!!!!!!!! And the Public Enemy website people have also put it on their website, which now means PE are now supporting a cross-platform non-proprietary format!!!!!!! So it's ended up all right in the end anyway!!!!!!!
