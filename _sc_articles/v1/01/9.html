http://www.interact-acc.com/news/dex_drive_release.htm

This "Dex Drive" thingy apparently allows you to plug in the memory card of a N64 or Playstation and allows you to read and write from it on a PC, like it was a Zip drive!! Apparently console fans will be able to swap high scores and other things that "will revolutionize console gaming as we know it"!!!!
