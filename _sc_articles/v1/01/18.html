Oh yeah, the French did reasonably well too here I gather... Anyway, if you were sick of the World Cup, you'll be even sicker if you use MS Word!!! "W97M/WorldCup98" is another variation of the world's most popular strain of virus, MS Word Basic Macro Viruses!! This one apparently will add a couple of lines to your autoexec.bat to format your HD on the day of the World Cup final!!! Even wackier, on the day, a dialog appears asking what team you support, and if you pick one the virus writers like, you get a congratulations message!!! (It still does the dirty though!!!)
