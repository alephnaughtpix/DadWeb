Who owns the smiley logo?!?!??! Apparently it's some Frenchie guy called Franklin Loufrani who did it in 1968 for publications about the Paris student riots and trademarked it in 1971, but the guy that actually designed first it was an American called Harvey Ball who first did it in 1963 for an insurance company promotion!!!! So it's a bit of a not-so-smiley business going on!!!
