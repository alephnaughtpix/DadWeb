This is a reference to the claim that the assassination of Israeli PM Yitzhak Rabin was predicted in the Bible!!! Well, someone's taken him up on it!!!! And they've found loadsa people in Moby Dick, including Yitzhak Rabin, who were assassinated after the book was published!!!
