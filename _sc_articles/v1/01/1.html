Imagine if a computer a game could see how difficult you're finding the game, and adjust itself!!! Well, the guys at MIT are working on it!!! According to the link below, "researchers at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology's Media Lab are working on computers that can "read" a person's mind by monitoring body movements." Unfortunately, being the boring people that they are, they're using it for more serious stuff like "effective tutors" or video recorders that "sense your boredom and fast-forward through the dull parts of a movie", but I'm sure you can see the interesting applications!!! One prob tho- if Windows gets hold of that sort of technology, and reads people reactions to it, I can imagine it becoming really depressed, like a sort of Marvin the Paranoid Android of operating systems!!!!
