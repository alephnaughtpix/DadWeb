You know those adverts from EA Sports for their football games, where you get some cockney person going "Whahey!!! I'm mad about football me!!! etc", and then they have some American voice saying "EASports!!!It'sInTheGame!!!!" very quickly!!!!!! Well, you might ask, what is in the game!?!?!?! Well, in this case, it's the pilot episode of that popular TV show "South Park"!!!!!!!! Yep, someone decided to put a copy of the original South Park story "The Spirit of Christmas" on the CD-Rom of one of EA's games without telling anyone!!!!!!! If you've got the early Playstation version of "Tiger Woods 99 PGA Tour" then load the CD-Rom into your PC, and see if you can find it!!!!!!!!!! Just don't show it me, that's all!!!!!!
