--- version: 1 id: 173 article_id: 173 title: "Price Wars at Star Wars!!!" issue: 6 date: 1998-08-21 00:05:00 ---

Anyone jealous at the US getting the first look of the next Star Wars movie?!?! Well, don't be, because the fact that they're getting it first means that it might be more expensive for them to see it there!!! Apparently the fact that no-one really knows exactly how it's going to perform (Well, you never know, it might not do well!!! Aliens might land before May 1999, or a meteorite might hit New York!!!!), combined with the fact that it's most anticipated movie of all time, means that the cinemas might hike up the price just in case it's a dud!!! They figure that the people who be wanting to see it first won't be too fussy about paying higher prices!!!

At least by the time it hits everywhere, we'll know if it's a success or not, so the ticket price probably will have settled down by then!!!!
