--- version: 1 id: 156 article_id: 156 title: "Well, at least it's not the millenium bug!!!" issue: 5 date: 1998-08-14 00:15:00 ---

When Prove It 2000, a piece of software used to check other software for the millenium bug, was run on Windows 98, it said that 98 might suffer from a millenium prob!!!! Then the makers of Prove It 2000 checked more carefully, and found it didn't suffer from the end-of-millenium bug!!!! It suffered from an end-of-every-year bug!!! Apparently Windows 98 leaps two days forward or one day back when the clock ticks past midnight on Hogmanay (or New Year's Eve for non-Scottish readers!!!), for every year they tested!!!!!
