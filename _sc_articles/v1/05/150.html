--- version: 1 id: 150 article_id: 150 title: "Home Alone 3- on DVD!!!" issue: 5 date: 1998-08-14 00:09:00 ---

Well, looks like DVD is probably going to take off after all!!!! And the best sign is the announcement from Twentieth Century Fox Home Entertainment that they're going to release some titles on DVD!!! But the dead giveaway is the absolutely dire choice of titles- just like the early days of home video!!! Check this out: Porky's, Predator, Jingle All The Way, Marked For Death, Home Alone 3, and Hope Floats!!! The only decent titles in the whole bunch are The Abyss and Young Frankenstien!!! I suppose we'll just have to brave cheapo straight-to-DVD crap with the odd occasional gem for a few more years just like we did with videos!!!
