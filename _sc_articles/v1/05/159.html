Sony thought they were onto a winner when they did a camera that could shoot in night-vision!!! Unfortunely when this "feature" is used in daylight, the "night-vision" works out a bit more like Superman's X-ray vision- on people that is!! So an average shot of a nice family day in the park shot through this camera will end up looking like a rather blurry old pic of a nudist colony!!!!

For some extraordinary reason, Sony have recalled this wonder of modern technology!!! Shame on them I say- not that I'm a peeping tom or anything...
