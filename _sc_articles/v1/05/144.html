--- version: 1 id: 144 article_id: 144 title: "Your body emits light!!!" issue: 5 date: 1998-08-14 00:03:00 ---

Of a very low intensity though!!! Dr Gerard Hyland of Warwick Uni has found that your body's electromagnetic field might give off the odd photon, and this isn't actually a random thing, it's actually part of your body's natural functions!!! This might open a can worms, as this means that things which give off low electromagnetic fields (eg the microwaves from mobile phones and microwave ovens!!) might be actually interfering directly with your body's electromagnetic field!!!!
