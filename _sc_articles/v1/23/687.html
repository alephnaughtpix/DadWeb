Some of you young sprites might dimly remember that at the end of last year the Senior Dads multimedia empire expanded a little bit!!!!!!!!!!! We are of course talking about our brillibobs all-music-and-no-talk internet radio station, Radio East Milton!!!!!!!!!!! (See issue 18...) Now, some of you might be saying: "Well, your playlist of stuff like Abba, The Jesus & Mary Chain, The Beach Boys, Public Enemy and Bow Wow Wow is really wicked, man, but when are you lot going to do your own flippin' radio progs?!?!?!" The answer is- we don't have time to do a whole flippin' live radio station, you idiots!!!!!!!!!!!

But these are pretty exciting days for internet radio fans!!!!!!!! Not only are there big libraries of music like Imagine Radio, (Who do our radio station!!!!!) there was also recently Nullsoft's spiffy Shoutcast, which allowed anything that could be played on Winamp to be broadcast on tha NetWeb like it was a RealAudio server!!!!!!! Now here's a new one- and it's a "talkie" equivalent of Imagine Radio!!!!!!! And it allows you to add your own programmes!!!!!! This spiffy lot are called Radio Orang for some reason, and they've got heaps of programmes and various bits contributed by outside people!!!!! You can stitch all these bits together in your own playlist, and publish that playlist!!!!!!!!!! So, who knows!!!! Maybe, we'll be able to stich together our own playlist, add a couple of our own bits, and bring out Radio West Milton!!!!!!!!!!!! Not that we're promising anything of course!!!!!!!!!!!!
