Now, the impression you might get from some games "mags" is that people in the games industry have big egos, like they're rock stars or something!!!!!!!! They then to people that had already bought a full refund and free game, or patches and a discount toward buying the next version!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

Mind you, they are called "Sierra"!!!!!!!!!! If your company is named after a crap 70s car, then you probably don't have too many problems with your ego!!!!!!!!!!!!
