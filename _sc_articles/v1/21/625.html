Well, you might have heard of the VHS of new video media, DVD, but you might not have yet heard of the Blockbusters/Sky Box Office of new video media, called DIVX!!!!! Unlike DVD, DIVX "software" only allows you to play it for a limited amount of time, then you have to pay to view it again!!!!!

For some odd reason, a lot of people don't really like this idea!!!!! And there's been a lot of DVD/DIVX flamewars going on, and a lot of the pro-DVD websites are not too kind about DIVX!!!!!! One thing there hasn't been too much of though, is websites that are actually in _favour_ of DIVX- until recently, when a few of them suddenly sprang up from nowhere!!!!! Oddly enough, a few people on the DVD side wondered if the sites were actually made up by pro DIVX companies!!!!
