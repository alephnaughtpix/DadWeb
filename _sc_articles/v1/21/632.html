Here's another thing you might remember about the early 80s!!!!!! The BBC Micro we all know and love was launched as part of the Beeb's Computer Literacy Programme, another part of which was this telly proggy called "The Computer Programme"!!!!! (Funky name, huh?!?!?) The title sequence had some owl flying about whilst "Computer World" by those Kraftwerk dudes played in the background!!!!!

You might remember that, but do you remember the very first bit in the very first ever programme, just before the titles ran?!?!?! Well, I do!!!! It was this sort of "comedy sketch" thing, with Roy Kinnear as the bloke who ran a computer shop, and Fred Harris (From that classic telly show, "Leapfrog"!!!) as a really naive person wanting to buy a computer!!!! At one point, Fred wondered what colours the computers were available in, and we all rolled in the isles (Laughing!!!!) because we knew that you couldn't get computers in different colours!!!!!!!

Well, Fred has the last laugh now!!!! Apple are now releasing their iMacs in five different colours!!!!!!! So now someone who knows nothing about computers can now walk into a computer shop, ask for a computer in green, and we won't be able to laugh at them any more!!!!!!!!!!!
