Yes!!!!!!!! Someone's done Quake in Java!!!!!!!!!!!!! Well, not really, but most of it's in Java, and part of the really fiddly bits are done in machine code!!!!!!!!!!

If you have a reasonably fast computer and Netscape/IE 4, then this is a rather neat-o little game!!!!!! Only problem is that it's a bit slow on my 486!!!!!!
