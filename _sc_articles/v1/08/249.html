Well, he's been getting famous, and lots of people have been writing about him, so it was only a matter of time before there was a massive backlash!!!!! I'm of course talking about Harry Knowles and his movie rumours website Ain't It Cool News!!!! There was some rumblings over the news about Peter Jackson and the Lord of the Rings trilogy and Harry's enthusiastic reaction (Isn't he always enthusiastic!??!?!) and then the news that Peter Jackson was going to answer readers questions about the films on the site... Then his week his site went one movie rumour too far and posted some rumours seen in the UK movie free mag "Flicks" to the effect that the Star Wars movie had been delayed!!! As many people suspected, this was a crap mag just retelling an old rumour, but in the talkback section accompanying the article turned into a complete slag-off of the whole site, with loads of people going right ahead and calling a Harry "full of shit" and a "sellout"!!! Not that they're jealous of the site or Harry's new found fame or anything...

http://www.aint-it-cool-news.com/tb_display.cgi?id=2013#5916

Some of them are also posting under false names as well...

http://www.aint-it-cool-news.com/display.cgi?id=2027&next=1

Personally I don't think the site has changed that much since the revamp in story terms... Harry always used to go on about everything being "cool", and I'm pretty sure older readers remember the "ZX Shrink Chamber" story appearing the in the first issue of Senior Citizen- that was an untrue story as well!!! (See issue 1...) The main difference is the adding of the "talkback" section to the articles, giving free-rein to anyone that wants to slag off Harry and his burgeoning celebrity status!!!
