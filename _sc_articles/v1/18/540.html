Well, here's an internet music story that's also a non-MP3 story, amazingly enough!!!! At least, it's a non-MP3 story, according to the Internet Underground Music Archive, a really old internet site devoted to exposing (Oooh blimey missus!!!!!!) new musical talent, who have done a rather big report on the affect the Internet will have on music!!!!! No, they don't say that music on the Internet makes you more depressed!!!!!!! (See issue 8...) In fact, they say the internetty webby thingy will revolutionise the way music is distributed- which by an interesting coincidence, would make IUMA a major player, as they, well, distribute music on the Internet!!!!!!

But that's not all!!!! Apparently, MP3 is *not* going to be the major format used on the Internet!!!!!!! Which is jolly lucky for IUMA, as all the the full length songs in their archives appear to be in Realaudio or Liquid Audio format!!!!!!
