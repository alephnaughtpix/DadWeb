Those UK Teletext dudes are really on the ball aren't they?!?!?! On the day (18/11/98) their C4 mag on p143 announces that Billy Idol is putting some new material in MP3 form on http://www.mp3.com/, Sir William of Idol decides to remove the two tracks he originally submitted!!!! In this message on mp3.com's bbs, it is strongly implied by the admin that Sir Cyberwilly of Idol's label might have been put under a bit of pressure by the American record industry body (RIAA), who at the moment, don't really like MP3!!!! Ah well, all it means is that major labels will be waiting for ages for some proprietary format to be worked out, whilst everyone else will still be using MP3s... Sounds like they're really thinking ahead, huh?!?!?
