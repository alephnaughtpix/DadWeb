Have you ever looked at these webpages and thought "I think they would go better if you squished them!!!!!" Well, that day may be not so far away!!!! Apache, the world's no. 1 webserver (So it's like The Spice Girls of webservers!!!) has got support for sending compressed webpages, but so far no web browser supported it- until now!!!!! The Mozilla project to make the next Netscape browser has included it- and since text files compress really well, this sort of thing can make a really big difference!!!! Neat-o or what-o?!?!?!
