The writers in CNN-webland are getting in a bit of a strop because we're near the end of the 90s and no-one's yet thought of some catch-all phrase for the entire decade that's stuck!!! Well, looking at some of the "nominations", I'm not too surprised!!! I mean, how about "The Decade of Republicanism and common sense" - Yeah, means quite a lot to people like me in Central Scotland!!! Best of the lot isn't too good either- "Age of Information"- but at least it's a bit nearer the mark!!!

Let's try a couple of names ourselves... Erm, the "Netty" Nineties!?!? I heard on a radio prog about there being a "Niagra" of info on the web, so... "Netty Niagra Info Ninties?!?!" Erm, this is a bit difficult!!!
