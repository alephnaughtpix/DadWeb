Who's this funny bloke?!?! Well, it's not really a who but a what!!! It's actually a wireless local area network being developed by Carnegie Mellon Uni!!! Apparently, the network is set up a bit like a cellphone type network, and can handle 2 megabits of data a sec!!! Now, that would be a pretty impressive network to connect your little palm computer to!!! The name "Wireless Andrew" apparently refers to Andrew Carnegie and Andrew Mellon, who, yup!, founded Carnegie Mellon uni!!!
