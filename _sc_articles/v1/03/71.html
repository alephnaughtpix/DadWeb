You know how you search for something in a search engine, and you get a whole load of guff back that's completely irrelevant but has the same keywords?!? Well, some company called Direct Hit (http://www.directhit.com/) is working with a few of the search engine to keep track of the sites that people actually select from search results, so that they can keep tabs on what's really relevant to people in search results!! Only prob I can see with that... what if different things are relevant in searches according to which country you come from or the language you speak and so on!?!?
