You might remember Robbie the robot in the file "Forbidden Planet" being delayed because he needed an "oil change"!!!! Well, if the film was made today, he would be delayed because he needed to reboot Windows!!!!! This is because there are now apparently a new generation of robots controlled by PCs!!!!!!!! They can do things like "serve you drinks, set a VCR, even vacuum your apartment. " I don't know if I like the last idea tho!!! I mean, if a robot was to race all round the house with my Dyson, whilst still connected to the PC, it would cause a lot of damage!!!!!!!!!!

It's not all housework tho... "One dentist in St. Clair Shores, Michigan uses robots to talk to children when they arrive for dental work. " Just as well they didn't use real people- that would just be too sinister!!!!!!!
