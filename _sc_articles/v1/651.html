--- id: 651 title: "April Fool: Your clock's slow!!!!!!" issue: 22 ---

What will you be doing on April 1, 2001?!?!?! Will you be trading jolly japes en route to a big space station whilst playing "The Blue Danube" on an MP3 walkman!?!?!?! Or maybe you'll be on a ship going to Jupiter, and playing April Fool jokes on a really intelligent computer, who will later kill you dead!!!!!!! But according to this article, what you'll actually be doing on April 1 2001 is fixing the clock on your Wintel box, if you have one!!!!! That's because the fact that April 1 on 2001 falls on a Saturday confuses one of the DLL files in Windows, and it forgets about putting the clocks forward on that day, and only sets things right a week later!!!!!
