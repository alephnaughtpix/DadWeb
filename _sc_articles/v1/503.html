If you can read this on the week it was released, then either you're on the mailing list, or you're using a bookmark, or you've read the news on the ad for this issue on Usenet!!!!!!!!!! How can we be sure of this?!?!?! Well, you we know you certainly get here through http://seniordads.home.ml.org/ or http://seniordads.atari.org/ , because neither of them are working!!!!!

Monolith, the lovely people who handle http://seniordads.home.ml.org/ have annnounced that all *.home.ml.org domains are down until 1 December at least!!!!!

http://www.ml.org/status.html
